About IJMB

IJMB is a one-year advanced level program that helps you gain direct admission into Nigerian universities without JAMB/UTME. It is moderated by Ahmadu Bello University and accepted by many institutions.

Why Choose IJMB

  • Alternative to JAMB & UTME
  • Direct entry into 200L
  • Mobile-friendly registration & online support
